Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

What makes Kriano Technologies different?

We combine deep technical expertise with a holistic understanding of business challenges. Our approach transforms chaos into intelligent clarity, delivering not just solutions but strategic advantages.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Simple analytics projects can be completed in 4-8 weeks, while comprehensive AI implementations may take 3-6 months. We always provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

Do you work with small businesses?

Absolutely! We believe in democratizing advanced analytics and AI. We offer scalable solutions and flexible engagement models to work with businesses of all sizes.

What industries do you serve?

We serve multiple industries including healthcare, financial services, retail, manufacturing, technology, and education. We're also expanding into new verticals like energy, transportation, and more.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support packages including maintenance, updates, training, and optimization services to ensure long-term success of your solutions.

Can you work with our existing data infrastructure?

We're experienced in working with diverse data environments and can integrate with your existing infrastructure. We also provide recommendations for optimization and modernization.
